Table
The <table> tag enables us to organize the information in a table.
Example:

Product Sale
Product Name Shirts Pants Shorts
Quantity 10 15 20
Form
The <form> tag can create a form to fill in on a webpage. Users can fill in their information and submit it to a database or a data server.
Example:

Membership Information

Gender:
List
The <list> tag allow order or unordered items to be listed on a webpage. When we need to give some examples, using <list> can make those examples organized.
Example:

My favorite food:

  • French Fries
  • Fried Chicken
  • Salad
  • Waffles

My favorite colours rank:

  1. Black
  2. White
  3. Blue-green
  4. Purple
Image
The <img> tag enables us to display a picture on the webpage.
Example:

Water Splitting

Link
We can use the <a> tag to insert a hyperlink to another page or other position on the webpage.
Example:
