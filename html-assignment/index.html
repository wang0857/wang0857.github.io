About Me


Learning background of Ruo-Fang Wang

I’m an international student originally from Taiwan[1] and graduated with a bachelor’s degree in economics from National Taipei University[2]. After graduation, I worked as a Marketing Officer for four years. When working in the field of Marketing, I communicated with many designers for different types of marketing materials, e.g., promotion websites, online application form, DM, eDM and content videos; this was my first time to know about interactive media and website design.


Start to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and come to Canada

After many considerations, I resigned from my company, and started to learn those basic website languages such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript. During self-learning, I was also searching for a related program to study with others because I thought that it would inspire me with more ideas to design a beautiful website. The second reason for me to study a second program was to have a relative learning background of website design. While I was searching for programs related to web design, I found Interactive Media Design program of Algonquin College. The course list of IMD program was amazing because it contained many types of media design. What’s more, there were many resources in the college to help students find a job after graduation. I thought it was exactly what I wanted, so I applied for this program and came to Canada for study this program ultimately.

“It is the choices we make that ultimately determine the outcome of our lives.”

Though I’m not sure what my future will be after I choose to study this program, I’m still excited to start a new journey to change my career path. I believe that I can learn a lot of meaningful things in this program and the life in Canada, and this is going to change my life. I wish that I can always remember my initial purpose to be here, overcome those obstacles, and persist in what I want to learn.

Finally, I would like to say to my new journey, “Hello World!”
